Game Rules

How to win?

Piece "1" is your king, keep it safe and kill opponent's "1" to win.

How to move?

  • The piece walks exactly(not less than) x steps, x is its number.

  • A step is a move form your current box to on of the neighbor boxes(left, right, up or down).

  • When the middle of the walk path stands a friend piece or enemy piece, this path is invalid, you can not walk though that way.

  • When the end of the walk path stands an enemy, enemy will be killed, no matter what number it is(that means your "2" can kill enemy's "3" althought 3 is bigger than 2).

  • You can not walk back to the position already visited in one movement.
